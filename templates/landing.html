Glioma

SEVERITY: HIGH

A fast-growing brain tumor that arises from glial (support) cells in the brain. Most commonly found in the frontal or temporal lobes.

Learn more →

Meningioma

SEVERITY: MEDIUM

A tumor that develops from the meninges, the protective membranes around the brain and spinal cord. Usually slow-growing and often benign.

Learn more →

Pituitary Tumor

SEVERITY: MEDIUM

A growth (adenoma) in the pituitary gland, which regulates key hormones in the body. Typically non-cancerous and treatable.
